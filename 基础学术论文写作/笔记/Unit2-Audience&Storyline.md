### 2.Recognize your audiences 

信息收集者 The field intelligence gatherer: 聚焦在摘要和结论，maybe introduction;   very likely to read your whole paper.

竞争者The competitor: 聚焦在引用和文献综述等

The seeker of a problem to solve: reads the discussion,conclusions; read your introduction as well, as they know little in your area.

困惑的读者（Solution Seeker），聚焦在方法与结论  Big knowledge gap; Possibly readers from a different area,looking for a method that can be used in their area.

年轻的研究人员The young researcher，聚焦在导论与参考文献部分  Big knowledge gap; will not make sense of all your writing.

#### 三段文本的区别：

text1:wikipedia 

text2:Science journal- Vertebrate of the year 2013(not original scientific article)

text3:Abstract of an original scientific article. Source : nature.

区别：

**differences in technical vocabulary：**text3: 有这个动物的拉丁名，抽象的名词（may not be immediately accessible to your  general science reader）；text1：far less of these terms  .并且作者用大多数读者理解的语言来解释； text2: 仅仅一个生物学的名词：ribosome

**作者提供背景信息量**：text1,有很多，给不了解本专业的读者提供； text3:较少，因为作者认为读者都已经熟悉了这些背景。

**一些概念的定义**   专业文献可能对一些概念的定义非常少

**语言的区别** text2: very informal elements , informal verbs,: text3: more academic language.

-> reflect the assumptions the writer makes about the typical reader's familiarity of the subject.

## 2.2What is your storyline?

### 1.学术故事

学术写作中需要一个完整的故事吗？

故事表达了我们的情感

Every story has six key story elements: **characters(s)**, a **setting**, **tension**, **action** , **climax**, and a **resolution**.

讲故事讲究 龙头 猪肚 凤尾

1.setting：background    question/problem

2.characters  methods

3.tension/action/climax     results  discussion

4.resolution

every research paper should tell a story.



example 1:

genomic and archaeological evidence suggests a dual origin of domestic dogs

基因组和考古证据表明家养狗有双重起源。





### 2.How to choose your best story line?

1.what story fits your results best?

2.what are the wider implications of your story?

3.what is the biggest question that can be answered?

4.what is the fundamental new understanding?

5.what journal are you planning to publish?

6.what is the audience of that journal?



### 3.Data→Information→Knowledge→understanding

富兰克林女士拍摄的DNA的X射线衍射图

沃森和克里克由此提出DNA是双链结构

Data→Information→Knowledge(==your results==)→understanding(==out of the results      interpretation==)

一个完整的科学故事，一定是从knowledge再往下推一步，你到底从数据信息和知识有什么新的理解，也就是学术写作中的conclusions，
